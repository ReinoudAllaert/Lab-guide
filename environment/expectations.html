Expectations of lab engagement

Lab meetings

  1. All researchers in the lab are expected to attend the bi-weekly lab meetings, except in cases of unavoidable conflict. It generally lasts 1-2 hours.
  2. At the beginning of each meeting, all the members are invited to share the main activities of the past two weeks.
  3. This progress overview is followed by activities such as a presentation of research projects or data; a talk by an external speakers; or a discussion of specific research papers. Each meeting, another lab member will be in charge of organising such an activity. They can decide whether they want to present something themselves or invite others. The rotating schedule will be determined at the beginning of the term.

Communication within the lab

  1. Dr. Verbruggen relies primarily upon email for communication, and generally responds quickly to emails unless traveling or away from work. Only non-urgent or non-work-related messages can be shared via other channels (such as the lab’s Whatapp group for ‘fun activities’).
